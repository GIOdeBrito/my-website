About me

Ever since I was a little boy, technology has always fascinated me, from simple remotely controled toy cars, to more complex machines such as gaming consoles and computers.

Today I am a self-learned web and desktop developer, studying and practicing, either for fun or for learning, basically non-stop since my 16 years old. When I am not programming I enjoy reading history and fantasy books.
